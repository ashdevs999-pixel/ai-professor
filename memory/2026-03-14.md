### 2. Filed 5 AI Agent Ideas

**Idea #1: Knowledge Transfer Agent**
- **Pain point:** Employee knowledge loss when people leave
- **Solution:** Exit interviews + event-triggered knowledge capture (not continuous, not exit-only)
- **Halfway point:** Triggered by role changes, vacations, project completion, quarterly check-ins
- **Competitive gap:** Exit tools capture "why leaving", not "what you know"; knowledge platforms are static wikis
- **Revenue:** $49-499/month SaaS
- **MVP:** 2-3 weeks, <$1K

**Idea #2: Grant Writing Agent** ⭐ (My top pick)
- **Pain point:** Nonprofits spend 10-40 hours per grant, 95%+ rejection rate
- **Solution:** End-to-end grant automation (discovery → research → write → submit → track)
- **Competitive gap:** Databases list grants but don't write; AI writers are generic; no one connects all steps
- **Revenue:** $99-499/month SaaS, or 5-10% success fee
- **MVP:** 3-4 weeks, <$1.5K
- **Why top pick:** Clearest pain point, most willing buyers, least crowded, measurable ROI

**Idea #3: Vendor Negotiation Agent**
- **Pain point:** Companies overspend 30% on SaaS contracts, most don't negotiate
- **Solution:** Contract monitoring + market rate research + negotiation email drafts + tracking
- **Competitive gap:** SaaS management tools track but don't negotiate; procurement tools are enterprise-only
- **Revenue:** $99-999/month SaaS, or 10-20% of savings
- **MVP:** 2-3 weeks, <$1K
- **Risk:** Enterprise space crowded

**Idea #4: Tech Career Guidance Agent**
- **Pain point:** Tech professionals confused about career paths, skills, transitions
- **Solution:** AI career coach + technical expert + market intelligence + personalized learning paths
- **Target users:** Students, mid-career, career switchers, tech enthusiasts, developers
- **Competitive gap:** General AI is too generic; career coaches are expensive ($5K-15K); no one combines career + technical expertise
- **Revenue:** $29-99/month B2C, $199/user/year B2B, courses, job board
- **MVP:** 3-4 weeks, <$2K
- **Why good:** Large market (30M+ tech professionals), recurring need, multiple revenue streams

**Idea #5: AI Property Investment Agent** (Singapore → APAC → Global)
- **Pain point:** Property buyers struggle with "Is this worth S$1.5M?", no personalized investment advice
- **Solution:** Property analysis + market intelligence + personalized recommendations + financial planning
- **Singapore-first strategy:**
  - Perfect test market (property-obsessed, $30B+ annual transactions, tech-savvy, small, English-speaking)
  - Validate product quickly (5.5M people)
- **APAC expansion:** Malaysia → Thailand → Hong Kong → Taiwan → Australia → Global
- **Competitive gap:** No AI agent for individual property investors in APAC; platforms are listings-only
- **Revenue:** S$49-149/month B2C, S$199/month B2B (agents, developers), or 0.5% success fee
- **MVP:** 4-6 weeks, <$3K
- **Risk:** Regulatory (financial advice license?), data access (PropertyGuru/99.co may block scraping)
